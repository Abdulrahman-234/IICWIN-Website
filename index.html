    Our Objectives

    Awareness creation.

    To provide accurate information on all aspects of public health to mobilize and sensitize the community, with the aim of fighting ignorance, disease, and poverty.

    Health Charities.

    To provide access to comprehensive community-based health and protection services for women, children, adolescents and youths.


    Community Services.

    To strengthen community capacity by utilizing existing structures to respond to endemic and emerging health, poverty, and development issues.

    Community Groups.

    To develop a local community-based response to the growing needs of the vulnerable children, women, families, and other vulnerable groups in the under-served communities.

    Collaboration.

    To collaborate with other stakeholders for coordinated responses to maximize the effect of interventions in communities.



    Child development.

    To protect and develop children.
